The Story of
Maître D’

Founded by Julian and Elena Thorne in the autumn of 1994, Maître D’ was envisioned as a sanctuary where the rigorous discipline of French culinary technique meets the warmth of Mediterranean hospitality.

What began as a quiet corner bistro in the heart of the city has evolved into a multi-generational legacy, consistently redefining the boundaries of the fine dining experience without ever losing its soul.

The Visionary

Executive Chef
Adriana Valez

With a background honed in the Michelin-starred kitchens of Lyon and San Sebastian, Chef Adriana brings a technical precision that is balanced by an obsession with seasonal purity.

Her culinary philosophy is rooted in the belief that the chef's hand should be felt but never seen—enhancing the natural brilliance of the ingredient rather than obscuring it.
